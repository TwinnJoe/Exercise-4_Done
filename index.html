At Home Hair Care Tips

Give your hair the best treatment at home.


Apply a hair mask once a week

Hair masks are popular for their intense moisturising properties. They do what most conditioners can’t i.e. can provide an intensive conditioning and help to strengthen the hair fibre and provide the nourishment that your hair and scalp needs for strong strands.

Don’t Skip Washes

It might be tempting to skip washes because no one will be seeing you or your hair for a while. But we strongly encourage that you keep washing your hair regularly to keep is clean, smooth and looking its best.

Braid your hair

Perhaps you don’t want to comb your hair every day, but you want to keep your hair neat and out of your face. Now would be a good time to braid your hair.
